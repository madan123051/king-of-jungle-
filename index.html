How to Play

🌿 Master the jungle 🌿

1

👆 Tap or Swipe an Animal

Touch any animal tile and swipe in any direction — up, down, left or right — to move it.

2

🐯 Match 3 or More

Line up 3 or more of the same animal in a row or column to eliminate them and score points.

3

🔥 Chain Combos

When matches create new matches automatically, you get a combo multiplier for massive points!

4

🏆 Reach the Target

Each level has a score target. Reach it before your moves run out to advance to the next level.

5

👑 Become the King

Complete all 10 levels and claim your throne as the ultimate King of the Jungle!
