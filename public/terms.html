About the Game

King of Jungle is a free-to-play, browser-based match-3 puzzle game developed by WildSaura. The game features:

  • 100 progressively challenging levels across jungle-themed zones
  • Nepal wildlife-inspired animal tiles including Tigers, Elephants, Rhinos, Peacocks, Eagles, and Monkeys
  • Special Power Animal Tiles: 🦁 Lion, 🦒 Giraffe, 🦏 Rhino, and 🐆 Leopard
  • Global leaderboard powered by Supabase
  • Progressive Web App (PWA) for offline play on Android and iOS
