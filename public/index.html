How to Play

🌿 Master the jungle 🌿

1

👆 Tap an Animal

Tap any animal tile to select it. A gold border will highlight your selection.

2

🔄 Swap with Neighbour

Tap an adjacent animal to swap them. Only valid swaps that create a match are allowed.

3

🐯 Match 3 or More

Line up 3 or more of the same animal in a row or column to eliminate them and score points.

4

🔥 Chain Combos

When matches create new matches automatically, you get a combo multiplier for massive points!

5

👑 Become the King

Reach the score target before moves run out. Complete all 100 levels to rule the jungle!
